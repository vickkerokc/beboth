Mission

Showcase and develop ideas and strategies that policy makers can implement.

What issues are our mission-focused areas? 

  • Education
  • Environment
  • Healthcare
  • Energy
  • Food Industry 

How will we accomplish our mission? 

  • Showcase the issues, problems, pain points that need solutions
  • Research solutions
  • Showcase grass roots efforts already working on solutions
  • Develop lean processes
  • Bottom up and top down 

Who do we need to accomplish our mission? 

  • Vloggers and bloggers who are experts in one of the subjects. 
    These writers will shed a light on the issues
  • Investigative journalists
    These writers will create the process map for the current state of the systems
  • Business owners, Entrepreneurs, Managers, Leaders
    These creative problem solvers will develop new solutions and do it effectively and efficiently
  • Front line, grass roots folks doing the work that want to be part of implementing solutions and beta testing at their local community level.
    These heroes are who we serve. We will listen to the issues you face, hear your ideas to solve them and take notes.

How will we measure success?

  • Focus on results by defining metrics that measure success
  • Better service to each ultimate customer – each citizen
  • Better transparency to our shareholders – the taxpayer
